Way Forward

Governments and policymakers must adapt labour laws to protect gig workers, since current systems were designed for permanent jobs. This includes benefits, rights, and security for people in non-traditional work. The future of work depends on balancing the flexibility of gig jobs with protections that ensure stability and fairness for workers
